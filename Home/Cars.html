Ceramic pro is a multi-layered, clear liquid nano-ceramic coating. When cured, this technology will transform itself on the surface to a permanent, ridged, flexible glass shield. Ceramic pro can be described as an additional clear coat, with 3 times the hardness and self cleaning properties.

Good Investment

The coating will protect the vehicles exterior and interior from deteriorating for a lifetime. The resale market value will therefore be substantially higher!

Looking New All The Time

Once the coating is applied you will see a mirror effect, glossiness and colour depth, incomparable to anything else! With proper maintenance your car’s finish will shine for many years to come.

Less Maintenance

The slickness of the coated surface doesn’t allow dirt, break dust and tar to stick to your car’s paint, wheels or glass. This makes washing your vehicle less labor intensive and less frequent.
